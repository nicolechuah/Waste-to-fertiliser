Food Waste Friday

Join us every Friday evening to help reduce food waste and create a sustainable future!

Event Details

Day: Every Friday

Time: 4:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Location: Community Hall, Green Valley

Contact: info@wastetofertilizer.com | +1 234 567 890

Why Attend?

Be part of a movement dedicated to reducing food waste and promoting sustainable practices. You'll gain valuable insights into practical ways to minimize food waste in your daily routine and learn DIY composting techniques that turn leftovers into organic fertilizers. It's not just an event; it's an opportunity to meet like-minded individuals who care about the planet, exchange ideas, and work together for a greener tomorrow.

Incentives for Participants

Early registrants will receive exclusive reusable tote bags as a token of appreciation. Additionally, participants have the chance to win eco-friendly items like compost bins and seed kits, perfect for starting your journey toward sustainable living. Showcasing your dedication to environmental sustainability, you'll also receive a certificate of participation. Join us to make a real difference and take home some exciting rewards!
